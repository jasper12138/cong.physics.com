Dr. Cong Liu

Now I'm post-doc in physics at Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya under the supervision of Dr. Cazorla Claudio and work on the quantum solids, such as Helium-4 and other analogous bosons, toward to the novel supersolid states (long and far).

I obtained my PhD in physics at Nanjing University under the supervision of Prof. Jian Sun, during which I work on constructing planetary models, such as Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Condense matters at planets interior suffer high pressures and high temperatures and the states of matters, such as superionic states and plastic phases, could affect the formation and evolution of planets, such as magnetic fields.

Research

My current research is focused on condensed matter physics and states of matters at high pressure based on first principle calculations, Quantum Monte Carlo methods, crystal structure predictions, and machine-learning potentials.
